What is a Trial Day?

A trial day is a day for you to come in, work for DLTx, and see if you are a good fit. We pair you with another person who is at or above your level, or assign you to a specific project depending on the role you want to apply for and your skill level.

I have booked a Trial Day. What do I need?

After someone from our team has contacted you to verify the details of your trial day, you will need to come to our office (located on Level 18 of 1 Eagle Street) at 9am with your laptop. Our friendly team will help you get set up on the wi-fi and any project boards that you need to be a part of to work efficiently. At 9:45, DTLx has a daily stand-up meeting, where you will get to hear about the projects we are working on and introduce yourself to our team. After that, it's time to get to work! You will be expected to stay until 5pm, if possible.

What happens after my Trial Day?

After your trial day, our team will evaluate your work and how you can fit into the mesh. If we believe we have a position available for you, we will reach out with an offer.
